# IFRS 9 Week 3 Learning Notes

This folder contains Week 4 learning material built for the IFRS 9 Learning Journey .




## Week 4 topics covered

- Staging under IFRS 9
- Delinquency, default, and impairment
- SICR triggers
- Acquisition scorecards vs behavior scorecards
- Month on books bridge
- Point-in-time PD vs through-the-cycle PD
- ECL formula: PD × LGD × EAD

## Learning outcome

The week explains how a bank moves from borrower behavior to staging and then to expected credit loss provisioning.

## Note

The notes are intentionally kept simple and focus only on the concepts present in the provided transcript.
